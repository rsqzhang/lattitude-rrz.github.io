Cloudiness

Despite causing significant changes to certain other weather variables, the distance one is from the equator (0 Deg. Latitude) does not seem to particularly affect the presence of clouds in the sky. While this lack of correlation was not entirely unexpected, this confirms that any changes in temperature are not affected by overcast weather.
